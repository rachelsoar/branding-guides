Overview

Brand identity

Soar is the parent brand.

An AI studio that builds products to uplift how people and organizations work. Soar is not a product — it's the company behind Workplace AI, Fleet of Agents, and Citizen Portal. The brand represents vision, craftsmanship, and the belief that AI should serve people, not replace them.

Visual Identity

The Soar look: light, open, human

Technology that serves people should look like it belongs in their world, not in a sci-fi movie. Think open sky, warm light, natural texture. The aesthetic is expansive and grounded — never cold, never aggressive, never "tech for tech's sake."

Visual mood

Open sky — dawn, clouds, horizon lines
Real life — cities, homes, classrooms
Living green — growth, gardens, organic texture
Golden light — sunrise, golden hour warmth
Quiet depth — dusk, reflection, calm moments

Video and photography direction

Light, airy, sky-forward imagery. Warm natural light. Real environments and human moments. Minimalist UI when showing product. No dark/glitchy/techy aesthetics. No neon, cyberpunk, or circuit imagery. See the video production guide for full details.

Do
Light, airy, sky-forward imagery. Warm natural light. Real environments. Minimalist UI when showing product.
Don't
Dark, glitchy, techy aesthetics. Neon or cyberpunk visuals. Abstract tech patterns or circuit imagery. Busy screenshots.

Typography

Fonts

Urbanist
Section headings at 24px / 700
EYEBROW LABELS AT 15PX / 700

Headlines, section titles, eyebrows, labels, navigation. Weights 700-800.

Body copy renders at 17px with 1.65 line height. This is how all paragraph text, descriptions, and long-form content should look across every Soar page and document.
Captions and secondary text at 15px / weight 500
Small text, metadata, and timestamps at 13px — nothing below 12px

No other fonts. 12px is the hard minimum font size. Icons: Material Symbols Outlined only — no emojis anywhere.

Voice & Tone

How Soar sounds

The visionary founder who speaks plainly. Ambitious but not grandiose. Confident but not arrogant. We talk about AI the way a builder talks about their craft — with respect for the work and clarity about why it matters.

We are
Visionary — we see where AI is going
Clear — we explain complex things simply
Warm — technology serves people
Grounded — we build real things that work
We are not
Hype-driven — we don't chase trends
Jargon-heavy — no hiding behind buzzwords
Cold — we don't optimize "users"
Vaporware — we don't promise what doesn't exist
