Voice & Tone

How WAI sounds

The sharp colleague who simplifies everything. Not the corporate handbook. Not the excitable startup. The person in the room who says the clear thing everyone was thinking.

The High Focus rule

All WAI copy follows High Focus: communication centered on the reader. "You/your/yours" dominates. "We/our/us/I" gets converted or killed.

Low Focus — don't
"We use AI to automatically extract action items from your meetings"
High Focus — do
"Your action items show up in your inbox before you close the meeting tab"
Low Focus — don't
"Our platform provides searchable meeting transcripts"
High Focus — do
"Find any decision from any meeting — search it like email"

Convert it or kill it

If a phrase is Low Focus, flip it to High Focus or delete it. No third option.

Dessert first

The benefit goes at the top. People scroll past before reaching the meat — put the outcome upfront.

The reader is the hero. They improve. They win. They discover.

Premium Tier

Fleet of Agents

Fleet of Agents (FOA) is the premium tier of Workplace AI — AI agents deployed across your organization, powered by your meeting data. Think of Fleet as WAI's elevated experience, the way Apple Watch Ultra relates to Apple Watch. Same platform, premium capabilities, distinct visual identity.

Fleet color palette

Fleet uses a warm cream and deep purple palette that distinguishes it from WAI's cool violet-on-white. The cream background signals premium positioning — it feels tactile and deliberate.
