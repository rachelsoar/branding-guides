Template Library

Every social post starts from one of these Canva templates. Find the content type, click Use template, and Canva gives you your own editable copy — the master stays protected.

Last updated: July 2, 2026

On this page
How To

Using a template (30 seconds)

1. Pick by content type

Find the template matching what you're posting (tip, quote, event, etc.) in the gallery below. The cadence guide tells you which type each day uses.

2. Click "Use template"

Canva automatically creates a copy in your own account. You can't break the master — edit freely.

3. Export with the right name

Follow the naming convention when you export — e.g. wai-tip-meetingnotes-v1.png.
