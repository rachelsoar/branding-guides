Citizen Portal
Brand Guide

Brand identity, color palette, voice rules, and segment messaging for the Citizen Portal civic transparency platform.

Last updated: July 1, 2026

On this page
Overview

Brand identity

Insurance against being blindsided.

Citizen Portal gives citizens access to public spending data, elected official contact, and legislative tracking for their jurisdiction. Not a search tool — a way for citizens to stay informed about what their government is doing with their money and their representation.

Color Palette

Typography

Fonts

System UI
Section headings at 24px / 700
EYEBROW LABELS AT 13PX / 600

No web fonts — system-ui only. Fast, accessible, works on government networks.

Body copy renders at 17px with 1.65 line height. This is how all paragraph text appears across Citizen Portal pages and documents.
Small text, metadata, and timestamps at 13px — nothing below 12px

Voice & Tone

How CP sounds

Authoritative but accessible. Not activists — a tool for informed citizens. Factual, empowering, and non-partisan.

Copy rules

RuleDetails
Never say "democracy"Use "constitutional republic"
Never say "unlimited"Avoid in any feature or pricing description
Tagline punctuation"Facts, not headlines" — comma required
No "whether you're _ or _"Sounds AI-generated and repetitive
No emojisMaterial Symbols Outlined only
