Citizen Portal

Brand guide

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Brand identity

Citizen Portal is a government transparency platform that gives citizens access to public spending data, elected official contact, and legislative tracking for their jurisdiction. The brand is built on trust, clarity, and civic empowerment.

Brand position

Insurance against being blindsided by government decisions — not a search tool, but a way for citizens to stay informed about what their government is doing with their money and their representation.

Voice and tone

CP's voice is authoritative but accessible. We are not activists — we are a tool for informed citizens. The tone is factual, empowering, and non-partisan.
